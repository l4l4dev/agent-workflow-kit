# Human Decision

## Decision

The Human owner approves `TASK-2.3/DEF-001` as the governing Definition for TASK-2.3.

## Scope and Limitations

This Human Decision governs TASK-2.3 only and approves `TASK-2.3/DEF-001` and its Acceptance Criteria. It does not approve the content of TASK-1.8 retrospective records, modification or commit of `CANONICAL_LIFECYCLE.md`, retrospective records for unrelated Work Items, or any change to prior history. It does not claim that prior Canonical Records existed.

Any uncertainty about historical facts, prior approval, record scope, or retrospective representation must stop Implementation and be surfaced for a separate Human Decision. This decision does not pre-approve the content of retrospective records that TASK-2.3 may later produce.
